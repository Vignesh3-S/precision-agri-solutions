About App

Crop Recommendation Using PAS

Precision agriculture is the science of improving crop yields and assisting management decisions using high technology sensor and analysis tools. It is a new concept adopted throughout the world to increase production, reduce labor time, and ensure the effective management of fertilizers and irrigation processes. Precision agriculture is a modern farming technique that uses research data of soil characteristics, soil types, crop yield data collection and suggests the farmers the right crop based on their site-specific parameters. This reduces the wrong choice on a crop and increase in productivity.
